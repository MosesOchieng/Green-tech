Pyrolisis

Invloves use of high tempratures of about 120 degrees celcius to dry and disentegrate food waste to constituent elelements after which they can be made into raw materialsfor other products with similar or relevant carbo chain.

Use of Georthemal power would make this process effecient and reliable and we could save the world a loss chain from food wastage.

Behaviour Health Model.

The idea mitigates food wastage as primarily a health consern rather than a normal occurence and adocates it to be tackled through a strategic health plan.

This are focuses on several models of behaviour and their health implications or returns and gives the general feeling that the way to reduce food wastage is through changing the pyschology of humans in a psychaitric way.
